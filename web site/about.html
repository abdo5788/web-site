About Libya

Introduction

Libya is a country in North Africa with a long Mediterranean coastline and vast desert interiors. It has a rich history stretching from ancient Greek and Roman settlements to Islamic and Ottoman heritage.

Tourism History

For centuries Libya's coastal cities were hubs of Mediterranean trade. In modern times, archaeological exploration revealed remarkable Greco-Roman sites that attract historians and tourists.

Why Libya is Unique

  • Extraordinary ancient ruins (Leptis Magna, Cyrene)
  • Striking desert landscapes (Akakus)
  • Rich cultural mix of Mediterranean and Saharan influences
